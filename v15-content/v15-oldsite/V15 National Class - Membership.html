Join the V-15 Class Association Today and Support your Class!

2010 Membership begins at the new year.

Benefits of Membership:

  • Eligibility to sail in regional Vanguard 15 championships, including the Vanguard 15 Nationals.
  • Voting privileges: eligibility to vote on important V15 class issues.
  • Knowing that you are supporting your favorite one design class in its efforts to improve class development.

What are my dues used for?

  • Organizing championship regattas.
  • Paying class administrative expenses such as website hosting and maintenance.
  • Advertising the class and the boat to enhance the class's exposure.

Cost:

Membership :$15.00
